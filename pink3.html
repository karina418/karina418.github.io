Fun Facts About Pink

Here are some interesting facts about the colour pink:

  1. In the 1800s, pink was sometimes seen as a colour for boys because it was viewed as a lighter red.
  2. Pink is often used in design to create warm, friendly feelings.
  3. The Pink Star is one of the most famous and expensive pink diamonds ever found.
  4. Certain hospitals and sports arenas have used soft pink paint to create a calming atmosphere.
