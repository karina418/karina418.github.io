Shades of Pink

Pink appears in many different shades, each one gives a different feeling.

Examples:

  • Baby Pink — soft and gentle
  • Rose — warm and romantic
  • Blush — subtle and calming
  • Hot Pink — bold and energetic
  • Magenta — bright and powerful
