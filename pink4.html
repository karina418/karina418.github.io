Pink in Nature

Pink is not just a colour seen in fashion or art its also shown all around us in nature. Many flowers, animals, and even skies show beautiful shades of pink. These natural examples make the colour feel alive and connected to the world we live in.

Examples of Pink in Nature

  1. Cherry Blossoms — famous in Japan during springtime
  2. Flamingos — pink birds that get their colour from what they eat
  3. Sunsets — skies often turn pink at sunset or sunrise
  4. b>Hibiscus and Roses — some flowers are known for their bright pink petals
